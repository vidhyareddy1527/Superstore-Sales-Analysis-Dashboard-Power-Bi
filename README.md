#Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
Identify top-performing products and categories.
Track regional and state-wise sales trends.
Understand customer purchasing behavior.
Monitor shipping performance and delivery trends.
Discover business opportunities for growth.

Key Insights
Technology category generates the highest sales.
West region contributes maximum revenue.
Some products have high sales but low profit due to discounts.
Consumer segment has the highest number of orders.
Standard Class is the most used shipping mode.
Conclusion

The Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard successfully provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing sales, profit, customers, products, and regional trends. Using Power BI's interactive visualizations and DAX calculations, the project transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

The analysis helps identify top-performing products, profitable regions, customer purchasing patterns, and areas that require improvement. It also highlights the impact of discounts, shipping methods, and product categories on overall profitability.
